Irvine-Tustin Chapter



André Lombardi

Chapter President

Andre is a rising sophomore at Beckman High School in Irvine, California. He dedicates the vast majority of his time towards music, the sciences, and helping the community with his strengths. Working with InterSTEM, Andre aspires to introduce and foster STEM learning for anyone who desires to pursue their passions. Separate from InterSTEM, Andre enjoys performing and competing in organ and piano, and developing his non-profit organization, Kreative Kiddos.

Priyanka Kalidindi

Chief Operating Officer

Priyanka is a junior at Beckman High School. She is passionate about biology and neuroscience. She enjoys competing in HOSA and Science Olympiad. Additionally, she serves as a board member for a tutoring service at her school as well as the Anatomy Class Lead of a nonprofit called STEMEY. She has a passion for teaching kids and fulfills it through these activities. Outside of school, Priyanka loves to play tennis, do classical Indian dance, and volunteer at her local hospital and through Second Harvest Food Bank. Here at Interstem, she is Vice President of the Irvine-Tustin Chapter and on the Podcast Team.

Audrey Lee

Editor-In-Chief

Audrey is currently a freshman at Northwood High School in Irvine, California. She is passionate about writing and hopes to gain new experiences by writing articles for InterSTEM while helping others find interest in STEM. Audrey loves to write her own creative stories when she has the time.
